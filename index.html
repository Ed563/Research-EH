Development of Cell Phones

Many of the early cell phones were extremely large and a task to carry around. In 1983, the Motorola dynatac 8000x arrived for the public. This was considered the first mobile cell phone because you could take it around places. But at that time cell phones had not been spread to the general public. “They were primarily used in the sales and business world, but not often for personal use like you see today,”- Kreg Jones. Early telephones were just meant for talking, but gradually different features were added like voicemail. Eventaully manufacturers realized they could incorporate multi-media purposes into the phones. In recent years, The purpose shifted from a communicating tool to a tool with many purposes. Cell phones today have begun to replace many different gadgets like cameras etc. Cell phones have developed rapidly over the last decade. We can expect that the cell phone will completely take over the market, and there is no telling what the cell phone has in store for us.
